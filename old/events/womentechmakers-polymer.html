Women Techmakers Manila 2015

Connect. Create. Celebrate.
March 28, 2015





Google believes diversity and inclusion is important for thriving vibrant communities. Focusing on women-inclusion is the first step in a larger effort to diversify the global Google developer community. The GDG Philippines community has shown great interest in this initiative, starting with GDG DevFest Women 2013 and Women Techmakers 2014 last year.
This year, GDG Philippines is organizing another Women Techmakers event on March 28, 2015. The goal of the event is to empower women to pursue their dreams and build tools that will change the world. There will be invited talks from different women speakers, code labs, and breakout sessions. Let's show more visibility to our Women Techmakers and inspire our next generation of women leaders and Techmakers. Join us as we celebrate also International Women's Month. Please sign up early and save the date. We'll be sending confirmation emails with the details of the venue and how to get there.
